Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find your answer? Ask us directly →

How long does ERPNext implementation take?

It depends on the complexity of your requirements. A simple 1-2 module setup takes 2-3 weeks. A full implementation with GST, HR, Inventory, and Manufacturing modules typically takes 4-8 weeks. We provide an accurate timeline during your free consultation.

Does KIDV Tech serve clients outside Gujarat?

Yes! We serve clients across Gujarat, Rajasthan, Maharashtra, Delhi NCR, and all of India via remote implementation. Both online and on-site training are available. We have successfully delivered projects in 15+ cities across India.

Can biometric attendance integrate with existing HR software?

Yes! Our system is compatible with all major biometric devices including Essl, ZKTeco, and Suprema. API integration with your existing HR software is also possible. Book a free demo to see it in action.

Is SEO included in website development or charged separately?

All websites include basic on-page SEO — meta tags, sitemap, speed optimization, and mobile responsiveness. Ongoing SEO campaigns (keyword rankings, backlinks, monthly reporting) are a separate service. Contact us to discuss your requirements.

How much does AI chatbot development cost?

A WhatsApp Business chatbot starts from ₹15,000. An ERP-integrated AI chatbot with custom conversation flows starts from ₹40,000+. Final pricing depends on your specific requirements. Book a free consultation and we'll recommend the best option for your use case.

Is support available after project delivery?

Yes, all projects include a free support period — Website: 1 month, ERP/HR: 3 months, Enterprise: 6 months. After this period, an Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC) is available. Emergency support is available 24/7 for critical issues.

How secure is client data with KIDV Tech?

Absolutely. All systems are SSL/TLS encrypted. Client data is never shared with third parties. Daily automated server backups are in place. Read our full Privacy Policy for complete details on data protection.

Does the system handle GST invoicing and PF/ESIC compliance automatically?

Yes! ERPNext includes built-in GST invoicing, e-invoicing, and auto-generation of GSTR-1/GSTR-3B. The HR system automatically calculates PF, ESIC, TDS, and Professional Tax. Direct filing to government portals is also supported.
