What is ERPNext?

ERPNext is an integrated Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) software that helps businesses manage all their operations in one place. From small businesses to large enterprises, it provides real-time control over every department with 100% data security.

Accounting & Finance

Manage your bookkeeping, tax compliance (GST), multi-currency accounting, and real-time financial statements easily.

Inventory & Warehouse

Track stock levels in real-time across multiple warehouses. Manage batch, serial numbers, and automated stock replenishment.

CRM & Sales

Manage leads, quotations, and sales orders. Track salesperson performance and automate your complete sales cycle.

Buying & Purchase

Automate your procurement process. Manage supplier quotations, purchase orders, and track incoming shipments.

Manufacturing

Plan production with Bill of Materials (BOM). Manage work orders, job cards, and production scheduling efficiently.

HR & Payroll

Manage employee profiles, leave tracking, and automated payroll processing with integrated attendance systems.

Project Management

Plan projects using Gantt charts. Track time logs, tasks, and project profitability in real-time.

Asset Management

Track company assets, manage depreciation, and schedule regular maintenance for equipment.
