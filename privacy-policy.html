Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 1, 2026

At KIDV Tech, your privacy is our priority. This policy explains in clear, simple language how we collect, use, store and protect your personal information when you use our website, mobile applications, and IT services. We are committed to full transparency — no hidden clauses, no fine print surprises.

01 Information We Collect

We only collect information that is necessary to provide our services effectively. Here is exactly what we may collect:

Personal Information

  • Your full name, email address, and mobile/phone number
  • Company name, designation, and business address
  • Login credentials for client portals or deployed systems

Technical & Device Information

  • Device type, operating system version, and browser details
  • IP address, access timestamps, and page interaction data
  • App crash reports and performance diagnostics

Business & Service Data

  • ERP, HR, payroll, or inventory data you enter into our systems
  • Documents, records, and files you upload through our platforms
  • Communication history for support and service delivery
We never collect sensitive personal data such as Aadhaar numbers, bank account credentials, or medical records unless explicitly required for a specific contracted service and only with your clear written consent.

02 How We Use Your Information

Your data is used strictly to deliver and improve our services. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Set up, configure, and manage the IT solutions we provide to you
  • Communicate with you about your project, support requests, and updates
  • Send important service notifications, invoices, and renewal reminders
  • Analyse platform usage to improve our products and user experience
  • Meet legal, regulatory, and statutory compliance obligations under Indian law
  • Prevent fraud, unauthorised access, and system misuse
We do NOT use your data for unsolicited marketing, selling to third parties, or any purpose beyond what is required to deliver your contracted services.

03 Data Sharing & Disclosure

We treat your data with strict confidentiality. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information under any circumstances. Your data may only be shared in these limited situations:

  • Trusted Service Providers: Cloud hosting partners (AWS, GCP, DigitalOcean), bound by confidentiality agreements, only as required to operate our services
  • Legal Obligation: When required by Indian law, court orders, or regulatory authorities such as CERT-In
  • Business Transfers: In the unlikely event of a merger or acquisition, with the same privacy protections intact and advance notice to you
  • Your Explicit Consent: When you give us clear written permission for a specific sharing purpose

04 Data Security

We have implemented multiple layers of protection to keep your data safe:

  • All data in transit is encrypted using SSL/TLS (HTTPS) protocols
  • Servers hosted on enterprise-grade infrastructure with DDoS mitigation and firewall protection
  • Access to client data restricted to authorised personnel only, using role-based access controls
  • Regular automated backups ensure your data is never permanently lost
  • Routine security audits and vulnerability assessments are conducted periodically
While we use industry-best security practices, no digital system can guarantee 100% absolute security. In the unlikely event of a data breach affecting you, we will notify you within 72 hours as required by applicable Indian law.

05 Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as genuinely necessary:

  • Active Clients: Data is retained throughout the duration of your engagement with KIDV Tech
  • Post-Service: Business records are kept for 7 years as required under Indian accounting and GST laws
  • Inactive Accounts: Data from inactive contacts or trial users is deleted after 12 months
  • Right to Deletion: You may request deletion at any time and we will process it within 30 days, unless legally required to retain

06 Your Rights

As a user of our services, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: Request a complete copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Correction: Request correction of any inaccurate or outdated information
  • Right to Deletion: Request that we delete your personal data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Right to Portability: Request an export of your data in a standard, machine-readable format
  • Right to Object: Withdraw consent for specific data processing activities at any time
  • Right to Complain: Lodge a complaint with the relevant data protection authority if you believe your rights have been violated
To exercise any of these rights, email us at amit@kidvtech.com — we respond within 7 working days.

07 Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies to improve your browsing experience. Here is exactly what we use and why:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function — session management, security tokens. Cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics Cookies: Google Analytics to understand visitor behaviour anonymously — which pages are popular, how long users stay.
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your choices such as cookie consent settings.

You can manage or block non-essential cookies through your browser settings at any time. Blocking cookies may affect some website functionality.

08 Third-Party Services

Our services may integrate with third-party platforms. These have their own privacy policies which we encourage you to review:

  • Cloud Infrastructure: AWS, Google Cloud Platform, DigitalOcean, Hetzner
  • Analytics: Google Analytics (anonymised data only)
  • Communication: WhatsApp Business API, EmailJS
  • Payment Gateways: Razorpay, PayU (when applicable for client projects)
  • ERPNext / Frappe Framework (open-source, self-hosted)

KIDV Tech is not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party providers. We carefully select partners who maintain strong data protection standards.

09 Children's Privacy

Our website and services are intended solely for business users and individuals aged 18 years and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 18.

If you believe a child has provided us with personal data without parental consent, please contact us immediately at amit@kidvtech.com and we will promptly delete such information.

10 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our services, technology, or legal requirements. When we make significant changes:

  • The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page will be revised
  • Active clients will be notified by email for any material changes
  • A summary of key changes will be highlighted on this page for 30 days

Continued use of our services after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

11 Contact Us About Privacy

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please reach out. We are committed to responding within 7 working days.
